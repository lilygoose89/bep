Terms of Service

Ordering

  • When ordering, the client must provide the drop-off and pick-up locations along with the phone number(s) of the person(s) the order will be collected from and delivered to.
  • If the order item(s) weight exceeds more than 2 kilograms, BEP will charge an extra 5 baht per kilo.
  • If the orders requires funds exceeding 1000 baht in order to collect the delivery item, a deposit will be required before pick-up.
  • If the order is specifically a shopping order, there will be a an additional charge fee of 30 baht.
  • When ordering a shopping order, please provide a list of all groceries along with a photo reference for each item to.
  • If there is more than one drop-off point, an additional 20 baht per additional drop-off point will be charged on top of the original fees.

Items of Delivery

  • We do not accept the collection or delivery of any illegal items.
  • We do not take responsibility for any illegal items which may unknowingly be in our posession. The responsibility of any items in our care are solely the responsibility of the party which requested the delivery.
  • While we will take reasonable precautions to ensure your items are handled with care, we do not take responsibility for any items which may be damaged while in our care and posession.

Service

  • We hold the right to refuse service and dismiss an order if a rider feels uncomfortable with the order. Should this occur when an order has already been picked up, he or she will ensure that the item is returned to its initial sender. Any payment already collected will also be refunded.
  • Please be prompt when collecting your order. If your order is not collected within 15 mins of the rider's arrival and notification, the rider has the right to leave or charge an additional 10 baht for every 15 mins spent waiting.
